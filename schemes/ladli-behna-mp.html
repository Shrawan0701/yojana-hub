Mukhya Mantri Ladli Behna Yojana – Madhya Pradesh (2025 Complete Guide)

The Mukhya Mantri Ladli Behna Yojana is a flagship welfare scheme of the Madhya Pradesh Government aimed at empowering women by providing direct financial assistance every month. This detailed guide covers everything you need to know - eligibility, documents, step-by-step registration process, benefits, latest updates for 2025, FAQs, and official contact details.

🔑 Scheme Overview

  • Scheme launched by Madhya Pradesh Government in 2023.
  • Monthly financial assistance credited directly to women's bank accounts.
  • Aims to improve nutrition, health, and economic independence of women.
  • Benefits extended to married, widowed, divorced women aged 23–60 years.
  • Targeted towards poor and lower-income families of MP.

✅ Key Highlights 2025

  • Current monthly assistance amount: ₹1,250 per eligible woman.
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) through Aadhaar linked bank accounts.
  • Scheme is state funded, no application fees required.
  • Application facility available at Gram Panchayats and urban wards.

📋 Eligibility Criteria

  • Resident woman of Madhya Pradesh.
  • Age between 23 to 60 years.
  • Must belong to poor or lower-income household.
  • Family should not be income tax payer or large landholding farmer.
  • Government employees (except low-level workers) are excluded.

🧾 Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (mandatory, must be linked with bank account).
  • Samagra ID (Family ID in Madhya Pradesh).
  • Bank passbook / account number.
  • Ration card or income proof (if required).
  • Marriage certificate / widow certificate (where applicable).
  • Recent passport-size photograph.

🖥️ Registration Process

  1. Visit your nearest Gram Panchayat office / Ward office during registration camp dates.
  2. Carry Aadhaar card, Samagra ID, bank passbook, and other required documents.
  3. Fill Ladli Behna Yojana application form with personal and bank details.
  4. Officials will verify eligibility and upload details to state portal.
  5. Receive acknowledgement slip / application number for tracking.

💰 Payment Details

Payments are made monthly via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). Beneficiaries can check credit status through their bank, ATM, or online banking.

🌸 Scheme Benefits

  • Improves economic independence of women.
  • Helps in child education, nutrition, and health expenses.
  • Supports widows and divorced women financially.
  • Boosts overall household income in rural and urban poor families.
  • Promotes women empowerment and participation in decision-making.

🔄 Updating Details

  • Beneficiaries can update bank details or nominee information by visiting Gram Panchayat office.
  • Important to keep Aadhaar and Samagra ID details updated to avoid payment failures.

📅 Latest Updates 2025

  • Monthly assistance increased to ₹1,250 from ₹1,000 w.e.f January 2025.
  • Online application facility launched on MP State Ladli Behna portal.
  • Helpline services strengthened with toll-free numbers and WhatsApp support.

🧠 Tips for Beneficiaries

  • Link Aadhaar with bank account and mobile number before applying.
  • Keep acknowledgement slip safe to track application status.
  • Visit official MP government portal for scheme-related announcements.
  • Report any fraudulent agent demanding money for registration.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: Can unmarried women apply?
Currently, the scheme is targeted at married, widowed, or divorced women aged 23–60.

Q2: When will I receive my first installment?
Usually within 1–2 months after successful verification.

Q3: What if my bank account is not linked to Aadhaar?
You must complete Aadhaar seeding at your bank branch before benefit can be credited.

Q4: Is there any application fee?
No, application is completely free of cost.
