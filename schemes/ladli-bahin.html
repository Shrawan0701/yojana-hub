👩‍👧 Mukhyamantri Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana – Maharashtra 2025 Complete Guide

Mukhyamantri Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana is a new state government initiative by Maharashtra aimed at improving the financial security, nutrition, and dignity of women across the state. This scheme is specifically designed to empower women from economically weaker sections, boost their financial independence, and enhance the overall standard of living of their families.

🌟 Objective of the Scheme

The Maharashtra government launched this scheme to provide direct monthly financial assistance to women between the ages of 21 and 60 years. The idea is to create a sustainable source of income support for homemakers, single mothers, and women from BPL families who often lack independent financial resources.

  • Ensure women have financial freedom and decision-making power.
  • Reduce malnutrition by giving women funds for nutrition needs.
  • Encourage saving and investment for future security.
  • Promote women’s participation in local economy and entrepreneurship.

✅ Key Highlights

  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) of ₹1,500 every month to eligible women.
  • Payments are made to Aadhaar-linked bank accounts only.
  • Special priority for widows, divorced women, and single mothers.
  • Transparent and digital application process through online portal.
  • Scheme expected to benefit over 1 crore women in Maharashtra.

👩‍👩‍👧 Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be a permanent resident of Maharashtra.
  • Women aged between 21 and 60 years are eligible.
  • Annual family income must not exceed ₹2.5 lakh.
  • Applicant must have a valid Aadhaar card linked to a bank account.
  • Government employees and income tax payers are excluded.
  • Only one woman per family can avail the benefit.

📄 Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Passbook / Bank Account Details
  • Ration Card or Residential Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Mobile Number (linked with Aadhaar)
  • Income Certificate issued by competent authority

🖥️ How to Apply

  1. Visit the official Maharashtra Government Portal (to be notified soon).
  2. Click on “Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana Registration”.
  3. Enter your Aadhaar number, personal details, and bank details.
  4. Upload the required documents in scanned PDF/JPG format.
  5. Submit the application and note down your application reference number.
  6. You will receive an SMS confirmation after successful submission.

💰 Payment & Disbursement

The financial assistance of ₹1,500 is disbursed every month into the applicant’s bank account. Payments are processed through the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system to ensure transparency.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Can a working woman apply?

No, salaried women paying income tax are not eligible. Only women from BPL or EWS households can apply.

2. Is there any application fee?

No, applying for this scheme is completely free of cost.

3. How to check application status?

Applicants can visit the official portal, click on “Track Application”, and enter their reference number or Aadhaar number to check the status of their application.

4. Can unmarried women apply?

Yes, unmarried women above 21 years of age can also apply if they fulfill the income criteria.

Note: This is one of the most ambitious schemes by the Maharashtra government to improve women’s financial condition. Make sure you apply within the official dates to avoid missing out.
