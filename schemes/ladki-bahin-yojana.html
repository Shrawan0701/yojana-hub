Overview

Financial assistance scheme for women in Maharashtra providing monthly support of ₹1500 to eligible women aged 21-65 years.

Key Highlights

  • Direct bank transfer of benefits
  • Online application process
  • Transparent selection process
  • Regular status updates

Eligibility Criteria

Women aged 21-65 years
Family income less than ₹2.5 lakh annually
Resident of Maharashtra
Should have Aadhaar card and bank account
