🔥 Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) – 2025 Full Guide

Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) is a flagship scheme of the Government of India launched in 2016 under the Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas. Its main objective is to provide free LPG connections to women belonging to Below Poverty Line (BPL) households, thus ensuring clean cooking fuel and reducing health hazards caused by smoke from traditional chulhas.

🌟 Objective of PMUY

The scheme was introduced to address health issues arising from smoke-based cooking, promote women empowerment, and protect the environment by reducing indoor air pollution. The government provides a financial support of ₹1,600 for each LPG connection to eligible beneficiaries.

  • Provide clean cooking fuel to rural and poor households.
  • Reduce the health hazards caused by indoor pollution.
  • Empower women by freeing them from collecting firewood.
  • Improve overall health and reduce cases of respiratory illnesses.
  • Promote a smoke-free environment inside kitchens.

✅ Key Features of PMUY

  • Free LPG connection including security deposit, pressure regulator, booklet, and hose pipe.
  • Financial support of ₹1,600 per connection.
  • Beneficiary has the option to purchase stove and first refill on EMI.
  • Beneficiaries identified through SECC 2011 data and other eligibility lists.
  • PMUY 2.0 launched with an additional target of 1 crore new connections.

👩‍👧 Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be a woman belonging to a BPL household.
  • Applicant must be above 18 years of age.
  • There must not be any other LPG connection in the household.
  • Must have a BPL card or be included in SECC 2011 data.
  • Priority given to SC/ST households, PMAY beneficiaries, Antyodaya Anna Yojana families, etc.

📄 Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card of applicant
  • BPL Ration Card
  • Bank account details
  • Passport size photograph
  • Proof of residence
  • Self-declaration for adult woman applicant

🖥️ How to Apply for PMUY

  1. Visit the nearest LPG distributor (HP Gas, Bharat Gas, or Indane).
  2. Collect the Ujjwala Yojana application form or download from pmuy.gov.in.
  3. Fill in personal details such as name, age, address, Aadhaar number, and bank details.
  4. Attach required documents and submit the form to the distributor.
  5. Distributor will verify the details and process the application.
  6. Upon approval, a free LPG connection will be issued in the applicant’s name.

🌍 Impact of Ujjwala Yojana

Since its launch, PMUY has transformed millions of households by replacing traditional chulhas with LPG connections. According to government data, over 9 crore LPG connections have been distributed under this scheme. This has significantly reduced indoor air pollution and related health hazards.

  • Reduction in cases of Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD).
  • Improvement in women’s health and reduction in drudgery.
  • Time saved from collecting firewood used for education and income generation.
  • Decrease in deforestation due to reduced firewood demand.

📈 PMUY 2.0 – New Updates

Under PMUY 2.0, announced in 2021 and extended till 2025, the government has targeted an additional 1 crore LPG connections to cover left-out households. Additional benefits include:

  • Free first refill and free hotplate for new beneficiaries.
  • Streamlined KYC process for faster approvals.
  • Special focus on migrant families without residential proof.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is the refill free?

No, only the first refill is free under PMUY 2.0. Subsequent refills must be purchased at subsidized rates.

2. Can I get more than one connection per household?

No, only one connection per household is allowed.

3. What if my name is not in SECC 2011 data?

You can still apply if you belong to one of the other eligible categories such as AAY, SC/ST, PMAY (Gramin), etc.

4. Can I transfer my connection to another state?

Yes, you can transfer your LPG connection to any state through the portability facility provided by OMCs.

Important: Beneficiaries are encouraged to regularly refill their LPG cylinders to continue enjoying the benefits of clean fuel. Some states also provide additional subsidies to encourage refills.
